Star Health and Allied Insurance

Star Health offers a wide range of health insurance plans tailored to individual, family, and senior citizens' needs. Popular plans include family health optima and senior citizen health insurance policies.

LIC Health Insurance

Life Insurance Corporation of India (LIC) provides health insurance coverage through Jeevan Arogya, a unique plan to cover unexpected medical costs and hospital bills for the policyholder and their family.

New India Assurance

The New India Assurance Company Limited offers health insurance policies like Mediclaim and Arogya Sanjeevani, which cater to the healthcare needs of individuals and families with comprehensive coverage.
